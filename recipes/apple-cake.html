Apple Cinnamon Cake

Light, Soft and Packed with Juicy Apples

This Apple Cake with Cinnamon is an eggless moist cake base infused with small chunks of apple and flavors of cinnamon as well as nutmeg. The combination of sweet apples and aromatic cinnamon creates a comforting taste.

Apple Cinnamon Cake with eggs generally involves mixing flour, sugar , baking powder, egg, butter and cinnamon. Grated apple or apple pieces are then folded into the cake batter for flavor, moisture and taste.
