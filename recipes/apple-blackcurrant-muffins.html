Apple Blackcurrant Muffins

The Soft & Moist Apple Blackcurrant Muffins

Apple Blackcurrant Muffins will not leave you without indulging into them. Each bite will leave lingering flavors in your mouth. Well, my family was bored of eating apples as a daily ritual just because it was a healthy fruit. As a result, I was frantic to break this fruit monotony.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Essence
  • Blackcurrants

Steps

  1. Peel and finely chop the apples
  2. Dust black currants with some all-purpose flour. Preheat oven at 170° C for 10 minutes.
  3. In a wide bowl sift in all-purpose flour, salt and baking powder.
  4. Now add the dry ingredients to the wet in two parts. First, add half portion of the all-purpose flour mixture and whisk till smooth.
  5. Also add the remaining part of all-purpose flour mixture, milk and vanilla essence. Mix using cut and fold method.
  6. Finally, divide batter into 6-8 cupcakes liners. Top with some more black currants and chopped walnuts.
  7. Bake at 170°C in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  8. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before removing from the muffin tin.
