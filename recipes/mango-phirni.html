Mango Phirni

Richness of Rice Pudding combined with the Lusciousness of Ripe Mangoes

Mango phirni entails cooking ground rice with milk, sugar, and aromatic spices such as cardamom and saffron. The mixture is simmered until it thicken to a creamy consistency.

The amalgamation of creamy, fragrant phirni and succulent mangoes creates a delightful culinary flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 Mango (ripe, juicy, sweet & non fibrous variety) puréed.
  • 500 ml full fat milk
  • 2.5 tbsp basmati rice soaked for an hour
  • Dry Nuts

Steps

  1. Firstly, heat milk in a saucepan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add sugar and keep stirring. At first the phirni will turn a bit dilute. Keep working on low heat and stir constantly.
  3. Now add the mango purée and stir. The phirni must be thick and creamy. Should take around 3-5 minutes. Adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar if needed.
