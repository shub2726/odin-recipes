Pumpkin Bread

The Soft & Savoury Whole Wheat Pumpkin Yeast Bread

With the onset of Autumn season, also known as Fall in the western world, these quintessential pumpkins are in their full burst ready to be harvested. When so many pumpkins are in abundance, it makes it worthwhile to prepare recipes with this seasonal crop for Thanksgiving celebrations such as pumpkin pies, pumpkin bread, soups, muffins and so on.

Ingredients

  • Semolina
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Honey
  • Oil & Butter
  • Pumpkin Puree
  • Milk Powder
  • Salt
  • Pumpkin Spice
  • Whole-wheat Flour

Steps

  1. In a bowl take 100 ml lukewarm water. To this add sugar and instant yeast. Cover and let it rest until yeast is activated.
  2. Firstly, in a mixing bowl take whole wheat flour, semolina, salt, milk powder, honey. Combine everything well. Add pumpkin puree.
  3. Mix well till a smooth dough is formed. You may use water little at a time.
  4. Keep the dough in a greased bowl and cover with cling wrap. Let the dough rest till double in volume in a warm place.
  5. When the dough doubles in volume, take it onto a working surface and punch the dough ball.
  6. Now very gently lift the side facing you and roll it into a tight cylinder and seal the dough on the edges.
  7. Keep the pan of greased dough in warm place for second proofing till double in volume.
  8. When loaf dough doubles in volume, brush it with milk, sprinkle pumpkin seeds, melon seeds and place in a preheated oven to bake at 200 degrees Celsius for 35-40 minutes.
  9. Lastly, when the bread is baked, place the loaf on a wired rack and brush with butter. Cut after cooling for 2 hours.
